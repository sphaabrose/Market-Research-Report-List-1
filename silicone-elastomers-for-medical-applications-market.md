<p><p>Silicone elastomers for medical applications are categorized into three main types: High-Temperature Vulcanization (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV is durable and heat-resistant, suitable for various medical devices. RTV cures at room temperature, offering flexibility in application, ideal for molds and soft components. LSR, known for its excellent flow properties, enables intricate designs and is used in molding processes. Each type provides unique advantages tailored for diverse medical needs, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
