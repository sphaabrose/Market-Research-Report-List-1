<p><ul><li>Mechanical Based</li><li>Chemical Based</li><li>Membrane Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-water separation filter system market comprises three main types: mechanical-based, chemical-based, and membrane-based systems. Mechanical systems utilize physical methods like gravity or centrifugation to separate oil from water. Chemical systems involve adding agents that allow for the coagulation and separation of oil droplets. Membrane-based systems use selective permeability to filter out oil from water, often employing specialized membranes that reject oil while allowing water to pass through. Each type serves various industrial applications based on efficiency and cost.</p></p>
