<p><p>The Off Highway Vehicle Hybrid Engine market consists of two main types: Plug-in Type and Non-plug-in Type. Plug-in Type hybrid engines allow users to charge the battery externally, providing the flexibility to operate in electric mode for extended periods, thereby enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. In contrast, Non-plug-in Type hybrid engines generate electric power through in-built internal combustion engines, relying on regenerative braking and engine operation to recharge the batteries, making them simpler and often more cost-effective.</p></p>
