<p><p>The micro reactor system market comprises two main types: gas-phase microreactors and liquid-phase microreactors. Gas-phase microreactors facilitate chemical reactions involving gases, offering enhanced control over reaction conditions and efficient mixing. They are often used in catalytic processes and petrochemical applications. On the other hand, liquid-phase microreactors are designed for reactions involving liquid reactants, optimizing heat and mass transfer. Both types enable scalable, efficient production while minimizing waste and improving safety in chemical processes across various industries.</p></p>
