<p><strong>The Aerospace Metal Hose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strip-wound Hoses</li><li>Corrugated Hoses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace metal hose market comprises two primary types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses feature a continuous helical strip of metal, providing high flexibility and resistance to pressure and temperature, ideal for applications requiring thermal insulation and mechanical protection. In contrast, corrugated hoses are characterized by their annular corrugations, which allow for significant bending and axial movement while maintaining fluid integrity. Both types are essential for various aerospace applications, ensuring durability and performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
