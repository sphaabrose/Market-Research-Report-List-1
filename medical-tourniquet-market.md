<p><p>The medical tourniquet market can be categorized primarily into two types: latex tourniquets and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditionally used due to their elasticity and cost-effectiveness, providing reliable compression for vascular access. In contrast, TPE tourniquets are gaining popularity for their hypoallergenic properties and latex-free composition, making them suitable for patients with latex allergies. Both types aim to enhance patient safety and comfort during medical procedures, reflecting the evolving needs of healthcare providers and patients alike.</p></p>
