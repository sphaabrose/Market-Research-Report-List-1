Suction cup grab bars offer temporary installation and easy repositioning but may not be as reliable. Floor-type grab bars are anchored to the floor for maximum stability, ideal for high-traffic areas. Adhesive grab bars use strong adhesive for installation without drilling, offering convenience, especially in rental spaces. Each type addresses varying safety and mobility requirements.</p></p>
