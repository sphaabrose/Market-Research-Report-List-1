<p><p>Cloud-based contact centers encompass various market types essential for effective communication management. Agent Performance Optimization (APO) tools analyze and enhance agent productivity and effectiveness. Dialers automate outbound calls for increased connectivity. Interactive Voice Response (IVR) systems allow customers to interact with automated systems through voice or keypad input, streamlining service. Computer Telephony Integration (CTI) unifies phone systems with computer applications. Additional markets offer diverse functionalities to enhance overall service delivery.</p></p>
