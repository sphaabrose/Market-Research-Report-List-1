Pendulum devices convert tidal shifts into mechanical energy through oscillation. Barrage systems employ dams to create a potential energy difference, generating electricity as water flows in and out. Other innovations include experimental technologies like oscillating water columns and underwater kites, broadening the scope of tidal energy applications and driving growth in renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
