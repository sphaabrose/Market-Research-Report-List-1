<p><p>The Natural Citronella Oil market primarily consists of two types: Ceylon and Java. Ceylon Citronella Oil, derived from Cymbopogon nardus, is known for its lighter, floral scent and high-quality characteristics, often used in perfumes and cosmetics. In contrast, Java Citronella Oil, extracted from Cymbopogon winterianus, has a stronger, more robust aroma, making it popular for industrial applications like insect repellents and candles. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
