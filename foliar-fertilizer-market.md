<p><p>The foliar fertilizer market comprises various types, including nitrogenous, phosphatic, and potassic fertilizers, each serving distinct purposes. Nitrogenous fertilizers promote leaf growth and green foliage, while phosphatic fertilizers enhance root development and flowering. Potassic fertilizers strengthen plants' resistance and improve fruit quality. Additionally, macronutrients like calcium, magnesium, and sulfur are essential for overall plant health, while micronutrients such as iron, manganese, and zinc support critical physiological functions. This diversity supports targeted nutrient delivery for optimal crop production.</p></p>
