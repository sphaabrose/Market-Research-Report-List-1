<p><p>The Automated Facade Cleaning System market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact cleaning robots. Contact cleaning robots physically engage with surfaces, using brushes or pads to remove dirt and grime. These systems are effective for stubborn stains but may require maintenance for the cleaning elements. In contrast, non-contact cleaning robots utilize technologies such as high-pressure water jets or ultrasonic waves to clean facades without direct contact, offering a gentler approach and minimizing the risk of damage to delicate surfaces.</p></p>
