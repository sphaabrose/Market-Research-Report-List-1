<p><strong>The Resin Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Coated Sand</li><li>Resin Bonded Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin sand market primarily consists of two types: resin-coated sand and resin-bonded sand. Resin-coated sand involves sand grains coated with a thermosetting resin, enhancing strength and durability for casting applications, particularly in metal production. Conversely, resin-bonded sand utilizes resins to bind sand particles directly, forming molds and cores used in various casting processes. Both types improve the quality of metal castings and are popular in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and heavy machinery for their superior performance characteristics.</p></p>
