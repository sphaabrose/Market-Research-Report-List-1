<p><strong>The Plastic Mould Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Moulding Moulds</li><li>Extrusion Moulding Moulds</li><li>Injection Moulding Moulds</li><li>Blow Moulding Moulds</li><li>Foam Moulding Moulds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic mould market encompasses various types of moulding processes, each suited for specific applications. Compression moulding involves shaping materials by applying heat and pressure, ideal for large parts. Extrusion moulding creates continuous shapes by forcing melted plastic through a die. Injection moulding injects molten plastic into a mould, achieving high precision for complex designs. Blow moulding is used for hollow items, producing containers and bottles. Foam moulding creates lightweight parts by expanding foam materials, ideal for packaging and insulation applications.</p></p>
