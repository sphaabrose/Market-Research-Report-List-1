<p><p>The Steel-Plastic Composite Geogrid market is segmented into two primary types: Biaxial Tension and Uniaxial Tension. Biaxial Tension Geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement in multiple directions, enhancing stability for applications like road construction and embankments. In contrast, Uniaxial Tension Geogrids primarily support load in one direction, making them ideal for retaining walls and soil stabilization. Both types leverage the strength of steel combined with the flexibility of plastic to improve engineering performance in various civil engineering projects.</p></p>
