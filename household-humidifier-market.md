<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Humidifiers</li><li>Direct Evaporative Humidifiers</li><li>Thermal Evaporative Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household humidifier market consists of several types, including ultrasonic, direct evaporative, and thermal evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, efficiently adding moisture to the air. Direct evaporative humidifiers rely on evaporation from a water-soaked wick, providing natural humidity without excessive energy use. Thermal evaporative humidifiers heat water to produce steam, effectively increasing humidity but often requiring more energy. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various user needs and preferences.</p></p>
