<p><p>Plastic waste management services can be categorized into thermosetting and thermoplastic markets. Thermosetting plastics, which harden permanently after being shaped, require specific recycling methods due to their resistance to melting, often being repurposed through energy recovery or specialized processes. In contrast, thermoplastics can be remelted and reshaped multiple times, making them more versatile for recycling. Both market types are essential in addressing plastic waste, promoting sustainable practices, and minimizing environmental impact through proper disposal, recycling, and recovery techniques.</p></p>
