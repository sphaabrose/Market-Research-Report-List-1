<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Resonator market comprises two primary types: Direct Input Port (DIP) and Surface Mount (SMT) resonators. DIP resonators are designed for through-hole mounting, offering robust durability and ease of handling, often used in traditional devices. In contrast, Surface Mount Resonators are compact and designed for high-density applications, allowing for automated assembly and improved performance in modern electronics.
