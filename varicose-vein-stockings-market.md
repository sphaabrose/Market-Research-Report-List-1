<p><p>The varicose vein stockings market includes two main types: knee-length and thigh-length stockings. Knee-length stockings are designed to provide graduated compression from the ankle to the knee, promoting blood flow and reducing swelling. They are commonly used for mild to moderate varicose veins and are easier to wear throughout the day. Thigh-length stockings extend compression up to the thigh, offering more support for severe cases and helping to prevent venous issues in larger areas of the leg. Both types cater to varying comfort needs and medical requirements.</p></p>
