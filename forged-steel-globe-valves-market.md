<p><p>The forged steel globe valves market is categorized by bonnet types, including bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet valves. Bolted bonnet valves feature a mechanically fastened bonnet, offering ease of maintenance. Welded bonnet valves provide a leak-proof seal through welding, enhancing durability but complicating disassembly. Pressure seal bonnet valves utilize a unique design that maintains tightness under high pressure, making them ideal for critical applications. Each type serves different operational needs and environments in industrial settings.</p></p>
