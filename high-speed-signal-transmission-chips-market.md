<p><p>The high-speed signal transmission chips market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Relay Chips**: These enable signal routing and switching for efficient data transmission.  </p><p>2. **Switch Chips**: These manage multiple data paths, allowing for dynamic signal control.  </p><p>3. **Distribute Chips**: These distribute signals to multiple outputs, ensuring consistent performance across devices.  </p><p>4. **Matrix Switch Chips**: These provide complex switching capabilities, managing multiple inputs and outputs simultaneously for enhanced signal management. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing high-speed communication systems.</p></p>
