<p><p>The aircraft seat frames market can be categorized into three primary materials: aluminium, magnesium, and composites. Aluminium offers a lightweight, durable option with excellent corrosion resistance, making it a popular choice for many aircraft manufacturers. Magnesium, even lighter than aluminium, provides high strength but is less commonly used due to its flammability concerns. Composites, which include materials like carbon fiber and fiberglass, offer significant weight savings and design flexibility, making them increasingly favored in modern aircraft for enhanced performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
